Author Information & Guidelines

Manuscript Preparation

Write acknowledgements in a separate section at the end of the article before the references, if any. List here those individuals who provided help during the research. It should contain the information regarding any research grant support or the assistance of colleagues.

References

The references should be cited in the main text in passing [1] or explicitly as in [2] with large brackets not in superscript or subscript. The full references should be given as below in the order in which they are cited. A complete reference should comprise the following:

  • An Article in a Journal: Author’s Initials Surname, Title of Paper, Name of Journal (in italic), year (bold), volume, issue, page no.
  • An Article in a Conference: Author’s Initials, Surname, Title of Paper, Name of Conference, Place, year (bold), page no.
  • A Book: Author’s Initials, Surname, Title of Book (in italic), Publisher, Country, year of publication (bold).
  • A Thesis: Author’s Initials, Surname, Title of Thesis (in italic), University, Country, year of publication (bold).
  • Patents: AB Inventor1, CD Inventor, (Holder), Country Code and Patent Number (registration year (bold)).
  • Online Document: J Cartwright, Big stars have weather too, IOP Publishing Physics, Web. https://physicsweb.org/articles/news/11/6/16/1

After Paper Acceptance

The final decision on publication is made upon recommendation of Editorial/Reviewer Board Members.

Publication Time

Author will receive soft copy of their Paper in 2-4 Weeks after payment of Article Processing Charges and submission of copyright form.

Publishing Method

All articles accepted for publication will be published only online. Authors are free to download their papers at any time, no reprints or any other format of accepted article will be provided.

Copyright Form

Each manuscript must be accompanied by a statement that it has been neither published nor submitted for publication, in whole or in part, either in a serial, professional and transfer of copyright form before publication of the work. Click here to download Copyright Form.

Article Processing Fee / Charges

Upon acceptance, Authors are required to pay a nominal pre-press charge. Article processing charges (APCs) are associated with only accepted articles to cover the costs of making the final version of the manuscript freely available via open access.

Article Processing Fee / Charges: 60 USD

Note:
1. Author should note that once submitted paper is accepted it cannot be withdrawn in any case.
2. Paper submitted to IJARERS is not published/ in communication earlier in any means print or online.
