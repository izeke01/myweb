Welcome to the Advanced Engineering Research Journal

International peer-reviewed journal for advances in engineering and technology research.
International Journal of Advanced Research in Engineering and Related Sciences (IJARERS) provides a platform for original research in engineering, technology, and related fields. Our mission is to publish high-quality, peer-reviewed articles and facilitate knowledge sharing among researchers worldwide.

About the Journal

  • Scope: IJARERS covers a wide range of disciplines, including:
    • Engineering: Acoustic, Aeronautical, Aerospace, Agricultural, Architectural, Automobile, Biomedical, Biotechnology, Chemical, Civil, Computer, Electrical, Electronics, Environmental, Food Science and Technology, Genetic, Industrial, Information Technology, Instrumentation and Control, Leather Technology, Marine, Materials Science, Mechanical, Mechatronics, Metallurgical, Mining, Naval Architectural, Nuclear, Ocean, Petroleum, Production Plant, Safety, Software, Telecommunication, Textile, Transportation Engineering.
    • Other Fields: Human Geography, Geomorphology, Physical Geography, Biogeography, Humanities, History, Political Science, Commerce, Business, and Management.
  • Format: Completely online, international, and peer-reviewed.
  • Objective: To disseminate original research and facilitate interdisciplinary collaboration.

Key Features

  • Premier source for high-quality engineering and technology research.
  • Focus on innovation, emerging trends, and future directions.
  • International forum for sharing research data and interdisciplinary ideas.
  • Fast, fair, and constructive peer review by experts worldwide.
  • Articles authored by researchers from diverse backgrounds and countries.

Submission Guidelines

  • Articles must be original and not under consideration elsewhere.
  • Manuscripts should follow the journal's style and formatting requirements.
  • All submissions are subject to review and editing.
