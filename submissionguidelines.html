Submission Guidelines for Authors

Understandings

When submitting papers to IJARERS, authors are expected to have read and observed the Ethics Policy of IJARERS and the documents referenced in it. Authors are also encouraged to read and observe the COPE ethical guidelines.

  • Authorship: All authors who have made substantial contributions to the paper are included in the author list (others involved should be listed in the acknowledgements). Any change to the author list after submission of the manuscript should be approved by all authors, including any who have been removed from the list.
  • Corresponding author: The corresponding author is responsible for communicating with the journal and ensuring all co-authors have agreed to authorship, order, and submission.
  • Originality: Authors should ensure their work is original. If using work or words of others, these must be properly cited or quoted.
  • Copyright and permissions: Authors are responsible for obtaining permission to use any third-party material. By submitting, authors agree to transfer copyright upon publication. Authors may reuse their published manuscripts in IJARERS with due acknowledgement.
  • Data access and retention: Authors may be asked to provide research data for editorial review and should retain data for a reasonable time after publication.
  • Research ethics and laws: Authors must ensure their articles comply with research ethics and applicable laws, including data protection.

Submission Preparation Checklist

  • The submission has not been previously published, nor is it before another journal for consideration (or an explanation has been provided in Comments to the Editor).
  • The submission file is in Microsoft Word format (*.doc, *.docx). Other formats will be rejected.
  • The paper is formatted according to the IJARERS template.
  • Please suggest three reviewers (name, organization, official e-mail) as 'Recommended reviewers' at the bottom of Step 3. Reviewers must have at least a Ph.D. The editor retains the right to decide whether suggested reviewers are used.
  • Author ORCID ID will be placed during submission. Authors without an ORCID can register at https://orcid.org/register.
  • All submissions should include a cover letter as a separate file.
  • Authors are requested to submit their manuscript by email to editor@ijarers.com. Papers must be in English.

Submit Your Manuscripts

Please download the template in DOC, DOCX. Upload your manuscript and cover letter as separate files. When uploading a revised manuscript, also upload a response sheet.

Manuscript Preparation

  • Manuscript must be prepared in MS Word and submitted as a single file.
  • Papers should be written in English.
  • Use reference management tools (Zotero, Mendeley, EndNote) and IEEE style.
  • Follow the author guidelines and use the IJARERS template.

Cover Letter

All submissions should include a cover letter as a separate file, explaining the significance and novelty of the work. The cover letter is confidential and read only by the editors.

Similarity Report

Manuscripts must be checked for similarity using "iThenticate" or "Turnitin". Articles with a similarity rate above 20% will be rejected. Upload the similarity report as a PDF along with your article.

Ethics Committee Report & Declaration

For studies requiring ethical approval, upload the permit or ethical declaration form from the relevant institution as an additional file.

Formatting/Style

  • A4 paper format, printing area 17.5 cm x 25.8 cm, margins: top 18.1 mm, bottom 20 mm, left 19 mm, right 16 mm.
  • Authors' full names, ORCIDs, and complete address information must be included. Corresponding author must be marked with *.
  • Abstract: 150–250 words, single paragraph, self-contained.
  • Keywords: 4–6, placed after the abstract, in alphabetical order.
  • Section structure: Introduction, Method, Results and Discussion, Conclusion, Acknowledgments, Author contributions, Conflicts of interest.

Figures, Schemes, and Tables

  • Number all figures and tables (e.g., Figure 1, Table 1) and provide descriptive titles.
  • Legends should be included as separate paragraphs in the main text.
  • Preferred image formats: TIFF, JPEG, PNG, GIF, or vector-based files. Minimum 300 dpi for bitmaps.

Reference Preparation

  • All references cited in the text must appear in the reference list and vice versa.
  • Use IEEE citation style with numerical references in square brackets (e.g., [1], [2]).
  • Use citation management software (Mendeley, EndNote, Zotero) with IEEE output style.
  • The "References" heading should not be numbered. Number all references consecutively in the order they appear.

Review/Referees Process

All submissions undergo double-blind peer review based on originality and quality. Authors may suggest up to three potential reviewers (with doctoral-level expertise), but the editorial team has full discretion in referee selection.

Revised Manuscript Submission

  • Highlight all changes using track changes or colored text.
  • Provide a point-by-point response to all reviewer comments.
  • Submit both the revised manuscript and response letter as separate files.
  • Standard revision period: 20 days from decision date. Extensions available upon request.

Proofs and Reprints

The corresponding author will receive page proofs prior to publication. Authors should check for errors, verify figures/tables, and respond to editorial queries. Authors may download and print unlimited copies of the final PDF.

Copyright/Open Access

  • By submitting, authors warrant the work is original, not under consideration elsewhere, and contains no plagiarism or fabrication.
  • Upon acceptance, authors grant IJARERS exclusive publication rights while retaining rights for teaching, dissertations, and institutional repositories.
  • All articles are published under Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International (CC BY-SA 4.0).

Copyright Notice

All manuscripts must be original and not under review elsewhere. Authors agree not to submit their work elsewhere during review. Prior publication of results in other formats requires editor approval. IJARERS publications are licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0, requiring appropriate credit and identical license terms for derivatives.
