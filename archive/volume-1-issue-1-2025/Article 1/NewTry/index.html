About the Journal

The International Journal of Advanced Research in Engineering and Related Sciences is a peer-reviewed open access journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research in all areas of engineering and related scientific disciplines. Our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and knowledge dissemination among researchers, academics, and professionals worldwide.

Journal Features

Open Access

All articles are freely available to readers worldwide, ensuring maximum visibility and impact.

Rapid Peer Review

Efficient and transparent peer review process for timely publication of research.

Global Editorial Board

Expert editors and reviewers from leading institutions across the globe.

Wide Scope

Covers all branches of engineering and related sciences, encouraging interdisciplinary research.
