Welcome to the Advanced Engineering Research Journal

International peer-reviewed journal for advances in engineering and technology research.
International Journal of Advanced Research in Engineering and Related Sciences is an international peer reviewed refereed Journal of Advanced Research in Engineering and Technology aims to provide a common platform to disseminate the original research work. International Journal of Advanced Research in Engineering and Related Sciences is a peer reviewed journal with a key objective to provide the academic and industrial group of people a medium for presenting original research and applications related to Engineering, Technology, Application, Humanities, History, Political Science, Geography, Commerce, Business, and Management. It is completely online journal. The mission of this journal is to publish original contributions in its field in order to spread knowledge amongst its readers and to be a reference publication. International Journal of Advanced Research in Engineering and Related Sciences has following features: Premier source of high quality engineering and technology research, focused on innovation, changes and future directions of engineering and technology. Source of the latest news, achievements and research inspirations in emerging interdisciplinary engineering and technology, as well as traditional engineering fields, including: Acoustic Engineering, Aeronautical Engineering, Aerospace Engineering, Agricultural Engineering, Architectural Engineering, Automobile Engineering, Biomedical Engineering, Biotechnology, Chemical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Environmental Engineering, Food Science and Technology, Genetic Engineering, Industrial Engineering, Information Technology, Instrumentation and Control Engineering, Leather Technology, Marine Engineering, Materials Science and Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Mechatronics Engineering, Metallurgical Engineering, Mining Engineering, Naval Architectural Engineering, Nuclear Engineering, Ocean Engineering, Petroleum Engineering, Production Plant Engineering, Safety Engineering, Software Engineering, Telecommunication Engineering, Textile Engineering, Transportation Engineering, Human geography, Geomorphology, Physical geography, Biogeography etc. International forum for the dissemination of research data with the emphasis on broad and cross disciplinary works from various areas of engineering, designed to facilitate the exchange of innovative and interdisciplinary ideas between researchers from different countries Excellent articles authored by researchers from all over the world, who appreciate our fast, fair and constructive peer review provided by experts in all fields of engineering. Articles submitted to the journal should meet these criteria and must not be under consideration for publication elsewhere. Manuscripts should follow the style of the journal and are subject to both review and editing.
