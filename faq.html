Frequently Asked Questions

Submission Process

To submit a paper, please visit our Publication page and follow the submission guidelines. You'll need to prepare your manuscript according to our template and submit it through our online submission system along with a cover letter.

We accept manuscripts in Microsoft Word (.doc, .docx) and PDF formats. However, the final accepted version must be provided in editable format (Word document) for typesetting purposes.

There is no submission fee for papers. Publication charges are only applicable once a paper has been accepted. Please see our Publication page for current publication fees and payment methods.

Manuscripts should be prepared using our template available on the Publication page. Generally, papers should include an abstract (150-250 words), keywords, introduction, methodology, results and discussion, conclusion, and references formatted according to IEEE or APA style.

Yes, papers previously presented at conferences may be submitted if they have been substantially revised and expanded (at least 30% new content). The cover letter must clearly state the paper's previous presentation history and explain the significant additions made.

Peer Review Process

Our peer review process is double-blind, meaning both authors and reviewers remain anonymous. Each manuscript is reviewed by at least two experts in the relevant field. The review process typically takes 4-6 weeks from submission to decision.

After review, papers may be: accepted without revision, accepted with minor revisions, accepted with major revisions, or rejected. Authors will receive detailed feedback from reviewers regardless of the decision.

Reviewers are selected based on their expertise in the specific subject area of your submission. Our editorial board maintains a database of qualified reviewers across various specializations to ensure appropriate evaluation of all manuscripts.

Authors may suggest up to three potential reviewers or request exclusion of specific individuals in their cover letter. The editorial board considers these suggestions but maintains full discretion in reviewer selection to ensure unbiased assessment.

Publication

Once accepted, papers are typically published in the next available issue. The time from acceptance to publication usually ranges from 4 to 8 weeks, depending on the publication schedule and any required revisions.

Yes, IJARERS is indexed in several major academic databases including Google Scholar, CrossRef, and other field-specific indices. We are continuously working to expand our indexing coverage to increase the visibility of published works.

Yes, IJARERS is an open access journal. All published articles are freely available online immediately upon publication. Authors retain copyright while granting the journal right of first publication.

IJARERS publishes quarterly issues (four issues per year). Special issues may be published on emerging research topics, following conferences, or focused on specific research areas.

Yes, we track and display article views, downloads, and citations. These metrics are updated regularly and are visible on each article's web page to help authors monitor the impact of their work.

Copyright and Ethics

Authors retain copyright of their work and grant IJARERS the right to publish and distribute the work. Authors are free to use, reprint, and distribute their work for educational and non-commercial purposes with appropriate citation.

IJARERS has a zero-tolerance policy for plagiarism. All submitted manuscripts are checked using plagiarism detection software. Manuscripts found to contain plagiarized content will be rejected immediately, and authors may be barred from future submissions.

Authors, reviewers, and editors must disclose any potential conflicts of interest. These may include financial, personal, or professional relationships that could influence the research or review process. Declared conflicts are managed appropriately to ensure fair assessment.

We encourage authors to share research data supporting their publications, subject to ethical, legal, and confidentiality constraints. Authors may deposit data in appropriate repositories and cite it in their manuscript or include statements on data availability.

Author Support

While we don't provide direct language editing services, we can recommend reputable third-party services for authors who need assistance with English language editing. Note that using these services does not guarantee acceptance of your manuscript.

Yes, authors can track their submission status through our online submission system. After logging in, you can check whether your paper is under review, awaiting revision, accepted, or scheduled for publication.

We offer special consideration for early career researchers, including mentoring opportunities, detailed feedback on submissions, and potential publication fee discounts. We encourage young researchers to engage with our editorial board for guidance.
