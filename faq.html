Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: How do I submit a paper?
A: Visit the submission page and follow the instructions to upload your manuscript.
Q2: What is the review process?
A: All submissions undergo a peer-review process by experts in the field. Authors will be notified of the decision via email.
Q3: Are there publication charges?
A: Yes, see the Author Information page for details on article processing charges.
Q4: How long does publication take?
A: Accepted papers are published online within 2-4 weeks after payment and copyright form submission.
